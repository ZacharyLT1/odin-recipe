Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese
  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Grease a baking sheet
  3. Stretch dough to baking sheet
  4. Spread sauce onto stretched dough
  5. Spread cheese
  6. Place baking sheet into preheated oven for 17-21 minutes
  7. Take out of oven and let stand for 5 minutes
