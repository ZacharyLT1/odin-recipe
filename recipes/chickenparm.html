Ingredients

  • Breadcrumbs
  • Grated parmesean
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Chicken Breast
  • Eggs
  1. Whisk eggs
  2. In a shallow pan combine 1:1 parmesean to breadcrumbs and mix
  3. Pound chicken till 1" thickness
  4. Dip chicken in whisked eggs then in breadcrumb mixture
  5. In a shallow pan add oil and brown both sides of chicken
  6. Place baking sheet into preheated oven for 17-21 minutes
  7. 5 minutes before baking is done add sauce and mozzerella on top of the chicken
  8. Take out of oven and let stand for 5 minutes
