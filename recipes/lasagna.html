Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Italian seasoning
  1. Brown ground beef
  2. Combine sauce and beef until simmer
  3. Boil water and add in lasagna noodles; Cook till al dente
  4. Preheat oven
  5. Grease a baking dish
  6. Layer meat sauce then noodles
  7. Once you have used your meat sauce and noodles top with mozzerella cheese
  8. Place into preheated oven for 17-21 minutes
  9. Take out of oven and let stand for 5 minutes
